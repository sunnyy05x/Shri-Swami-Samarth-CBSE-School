Academics & Curriculum

Methodology aligned as per NEP 2020

Curriculum Overview

Holistic Learning Approach

The curriculum is thoughtfully designed to provide a balanced blend of academic excellence, life skills, values, and holistic development in alignment with the CBSE framework and the vision of the National Education Policy (NEP). Our curriculum focuses on nurturing young minds through experiential, activity-based, and student-centered learning that encourages curiosity, creativity, critical thinking, and innovation.

The school follows an integrated approach where scholastic and co-scholastic activities go hand in hand to ensure the overall growth of every child. Along with core academic subjects, students are exposed to art, music, dance, yoga, sports, computer education, Artificial Intelligence, environmental awareness, and value-based learning. This approach helps students develop confidence, communication skills, leadership qualities, teamwork, and emotional intelligence.

Learning Stages

Educational Journey

Foundation Stage
(Nursery To II)

The Foundation Stage (Nursery to Grade II) focuses on building a strong base for lifelong learning through joyful, activity-based, and child-cantered education. In alignment with the National Education Policy (NEP), the curriculum is designed to nurture curiosity, creativity, communication, and social-emotional development during the early formative years of a child.

The learning environment is safe, caring, and engaging, where children explore concepts through stories, rhymes, games, art, music, hands-on activities, and experiential learning. Special emphasis is given to developing foundational literacy and numeracy skills along with language development, motor skills, critical thinking, and values. Interactive teaching methods and playful learning experiences help children enjoy learning while building confidence and independence. The Foundation Stage lays the groundwork for academic success and holistic development, helping children grow into happy, confident, and responsible learners.

Preparatory Stage
(III To V)

The Preparatory Stage (Grade III to V) is designed to strengthen foundational learning while gradually introducing students to deeper academic concepts and independent thinking. In accordance with the National Education Policy (NEP), the curriculum focuses on experiential, interactive, and activity-based learning that makes education enjoyable, meaningful, and engaging.

During this stage, students develop strong literacy, numeracy, communication, and analytical skills through a balanced approach of academics and co-curricular activities. Teaching methods include storytelling, projects, experiments, group activities, smart classroom learning, and hands-on experiences that encourage curiosity, creativity, and problem-solving abilities. Students are motivated to ask questions, explore ideas, and actively participate in the learning process. Equal importance is given to values, discipline, physical fitness, art, music, sports, and life skills to ensure holistic development. The nurturing environment at Shri Swami Samarth CBSE School helps children build confidence, responsibility, teamwork, and leadership qualities. The Preparatory Stage lays a strong academic and emotional foundation, preparing students to successfully transition into higher stages of learning with enthusiasm and confidence.

Middle School Stage
(VI To VIII)

Middle School Stage (Grade VI to VIII) is designed to foster intellectual growth, critical thinking, creativity, and independent learning among students. In alignment with the National Education Policy (NEP), the curriculum encourages conceptual understanding, skill development, and experiential learning through interactive and student-cantered teaching methods.

At this stage, students are introduced to a wider range of subjects and deeper academic concepts that help strengthen analytical, scientific, and problem-solving abilities. Learning is enriched through projects, experiments, discussions, presentations, laboratory activities, digital learning, and collaborative tasks that make education practical and meaningful. Students are encouraged to explore their interests, think independently, and apply knowledge to real-life situations. At Shri Swami Samarth CBSE School, the Middle School Stage provides a nurturing and motivating environment where students gain confidence, develop strong learning habits, and prepare themselves for the challenges of higher education and future opportunities.

Secondary Stage
(IX To X)

Secondary Stage (Grade IX to X) focuses on academic excellence, conceptual clarity, skill development, and career readiness. Following the CBSE curriculum and the vision of the National Education Policy (NEP), the school provides a balanced and future-oriented learning experience that prepares students for board examinations as well as real-life challenges.

At this stage, students are guided to develop critical thinking, analytical abilities, problem-solving skills, and independent learning habits through interactive teaching methods, smart classrooms, practical experiments, projects, discussions, and technology-integrated learning. Special emphasis is placed on strengthening subject knowledge, improving communication skills, and enhancing confidence through continuous assessment and personalized attention. At Shri Swami Samarth CBSE School, the Secondary Stage nurtures responsible, confident, and goal-oriented learners who are prepared to excel academically and become capable global citizens with strong values and a positive outlook toward life.

Beyond the Classroom

Co-Curricular Activities

The school believes that every child possesses unique abilities, and co-curricular programs provide the perfect platform to explore and enhance those talents. A wide range of activities such as music, dance, art and craft, yoga, karate, public speaking, storytelling, drama, sports, quizzes, debates, cultural events, and creative competitions are regularly organized to encourage active participation and overall personality development. These activities help students improve communication skills, teamwork, discipline, time management, and self-confidence while fostering a spirit of healthy competition and collaboration. The school also promotes experiential and activity-based learning through exhibitions, field trips, club activities, environmental awareness programs, celebrations of national festivals, and social responsibility initiatives. Students are encouraged to think creatively, express themselves freely, and develop problem-solving abilities in a joyful and engaging learning environment. At Shri Swami Samarth CBSE School, co-curricular activities are integrated with academics to ensure the balanced growth of mind, body, and character. The school strives to create opportunities that inspire students to discover their passions, build life skills, and become confident, responsible, and well-rounded individuals prepared for future challenges.
