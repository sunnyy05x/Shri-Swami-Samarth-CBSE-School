Facilities

A Campus Built for Excellence

Our 8,855 sq. mtr. campus features state-of-the-art facilities designed to provide a safe, stimulating, and joyful learning environment.

🏫

12 Spacious Classrooms

500 sq. ft. each, equipped with TV, computer, CCTV cameras for smart, safe, and interactive learning.

💻

Computer Laboratory

Fully equipped 600 sq. ft. computer lab with high-speed internet and multimedia-aided audio-visual facilities.

🔬

Science Laboratories

Dedicated labs for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, and General Science with modern equipment.

📖

Library & Reading Room

A well-stocked library with a quiet reading room to cultivate a love for books and research.

🌐

High-Speed Internet

Campus-wide Wi-Fi connectivity supporting digital learning and smart classroom integration.

🏀

Sports Facilities

Vast playfields, basketball court, and skating rink to encourage physical fitness and sportsmanship.

🎵

Music & Dance Centre

Equipped with musical instruments and audio-electronic systems for creative expression.

🎨

Art & Craft Room

Dedicated spaces including a Clay Studio for hands-on creative learning and artistic development.

🏥

School Health Clinic

On-campus medical ward with a dedicated doctor for regular health checkups and emergency care.

🎭

Auditorium

A spacious auditorium for assemblies, cultural events, and extracurricular performances.

🧒

Children's Park & Activity Centre

A beautiful park and dedicated activity centre for toddlers to learn through play.

🛡️

Safety & Hygiene

Fire safety measures, 100% power backup, RO water filters, impeccably clean toilets, and locker facilities.
