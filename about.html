About Our School

Shaping Minds, Building Futures

Proudly CBSE-affiliated and committed to holistic education, Shri Swami Samarth CBSE School stands as a beacon of quality learning in Pimpri Chinchwad, Pune.

🎯

Our Mission

To create an extremely effective school that will empower every student to become a lifelong learner. Our goal is to facilitate the development of self-confidence, critical thinking, and a love for knowledge in our students — making education a joyful and transformative journey that equips them to achieve their ambitions.

🌟

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a school recognized for excellence in academics and for its meaningful contribution to the community — by nurturing well-mannered, well-developed, and well-informed personalities who lead with integrity and purpose in an ever-changing world.

Leadership Message

Message from the President

"Educating the mind without educating the heart is no education at all." — Aristotle

Dear Parents, Students, and Well-Wishers,

Welcome to the digital portal of our institution.

As we navigate through an era of rapid technological advancement and global interconnectedness, the role of education has evolved far beyond traditional classroom learning. Today, our responsibility is to nurture agile minds, empathetic hearts, and resilient spirits capable of shaping a brighter tomorrow.

Our institution was founded on the bedrock of academic excellence, character building, and holistic development. We believe that every child is unique, possessing an inherent potential to excel. Our dedicated faculty leaves no stone unturned to provide a stimulating, safe, and inclusive environment where curiosity is encouraged, innovation is celebrated, and values are deeply rooted.

We do not just aim for academic brilliance; we strive to cultivate life skills that prepare our students for the world outside. We achieve this by balancing a rigorous curriculum with robust co-curricular activities, ensuring our students grow into Critical Thinkers, Compassionate Citizens and Global Leaders Who respect cultural diversity and are ready to contribute positively to society.

Education is a shared commitment between dedicated teachers, motivated students, and enthusiastic parents. The trust you place in us is our greatest motivation. Together, we form a vibrant ecosystem that supports and challenges our young learners to reach their zenith.

Shri. Shriraj Babar

Chairman / President

Greetings to Everyone,

It is my privilege and honor to welcome you to the vibrant community of Shri Swami Samarth CBSE School. As we embark on another promising academic year, we renew our commitment to nurturing young minds, fostering a culture of continuous learning, and building a foundation of strong values.

At Shri Swami Samarth CBSE School, we believe that every child is unique, possessing a spark of potential that deserves to be ignited. Our approach to education goes beyond the boundaries of textbooks. Guided by the robust CBSE curriculum, we strive to create a dynamic environment where academic excellence merges seamlessly with character building, emotional intelligence, and physical well-being.

An enriching educational journey is built on the strong trinity of Students, Teachers, and Parents. We deeply value the trust that parents place in us, and we actively invite your collaboration, insights, and support as we guide our students toward their highest potential. To our dedicated faculty, thank you for being the anchors of discipline, innovation, and care in our classrooms.

I invite you to explore our website to catch a glimpse of our school life, achievements, and the joyous learning experiences that define us.

Let us work together to make this academic year a remarkable milestone of growth and excellence.
